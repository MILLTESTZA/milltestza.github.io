## 🎉 Features
reinvent-your-leftovers includes a range of helpful features:
- **Inventory Management**: Keep track of your leftovers and available ingredients.
- **Recipe Suggestions**: Find recipes based on what you have at home.
- **Meal Planning**: Plan your meals in advance to reduce food waste.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Designed for easy navigation, making it suitable for everyone.

## 🛠️ How to Use
After launching the app, follow these steps to get started:
1. Create an account: Sign up with your email to save your preferences and recipes.
2. Add ingredients: Input the ingredients you already have at home.
3. Explore recipes: Browse suggested recipes tailored to your ingredients.
4. Plan your meals: Use the meal planner to organize your week’s meals efficiently.
